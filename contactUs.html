Performance-Based Refund Policy

To encourage students to complete their courses, we offer a **performance-based refund policy** based on course completion and final exam results.

Refund Process

  • Enroll in the course and complete the full duration.
  • Participate actively in the course, including assignments and projects.
  • After completion, a final exam will be conducted.
  • If you pass the exam, you will be eligible for a refund as per the refund percentage of your course.
  • Refunds will be processed within **7-10 business days** to the original payment method.

Important Notes

  • Refunds are only applicable if the student **passes the final exam**.
  • Failure to attend the final exam **disqualifies the student from a refund**.
  • All refund requests will be processed only via the original payment method.
  • CodingCure reserves the right to update the refund policy as necessary.
