About Us

Welcome to CodingCure!

CodingCure is an ed-tech platform designed to help students and working professionals learn and master Java programming, Microservices, Spring Boot, and other technical skills. Our courses focus on practical learning, real-world projects, and industry-relevant skills to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience.

At CodingCure, we believe that everyone has the potential to become a proficient coder and a problem solver. Our mission is to empower individuals with the skills they need to succeed in the fast-paced world of technology. Whether you are a beginner looking to learn the basics or an experienced professional seeking to enhance your expertise, we offer a comprehensive range of resources tailored to meet your learning needs.

Our Offerings

Our platform provides tutorials and courses covering a wide array of topics, including:

  • Programming Languages: Master Java, Scala, and more.
  • Data Structures and Algorithms: Build a solid foundation in computer science principles.
  • Interview Preparation: Equip yourself with the tools and techniques to ace technical interviews.
  • Big Data Technologies: Dive into the world of Spark and AWS to harness the power of big data.

Interactive Learning

We emphasize hands-on learning through interactive coding challenges, projects, and real-world applications. Our carefully designed curriculum not only teaches you the concepts but also helps you apply them effectively.

Community Support

Join a vibrant community of learners and professionals. Engage in discussions, share your insights, and collaborate with others on projects. Our forums and chat features allow you to connect with peers and mentors who can guide you on your learning journey.

Our Vision

At CodingCure, we aim to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. We strive to create a learning environment that is inclusive, engaging, and conducive to personal and professional growth.

Join us on this exciting journey to enhance your skills and unlock new opportunities in the tech industry. Together, let’s code our way to success!

Business Model Canvas (BMC)

We follow a structured learning approach, providing quality education and a unique **refund-based incentive model** to encourage students to complete their courses.

  • Value Proposition:

    • Provide affordable, self-paced coding courses and tutorials for beginners and professionals to learn programming languages (Java, Spring Boot, etc.), web development, and software engineering.

  • Customer Segments:

    • Students, early-career professionals, hobbyists, and businesses seeking to upskill employees.

  • Revenue Streams:

    • Sale of online courses (one-time payments).
    • Subscription plans for premium content (monthly/annual recurring payments).
    • Bundled course packages (e.g., "Full-Stack Developer Bundle").

  • Key Activities:

    • Course creation, content updates, marketing, and customer support.

  • Customer Relationships:

    • Email support, community forums, and progress tracking for learners.

  • Channels:

    • Website (codingcure.com), social media, and partnerships with educational platforms.

Use Case for Payment Gateway

To make payments seamless and secure, CodingCure integrates **Razorpay** as its payment gateway. Here’s how it works:

  • Accept payments for online courses, subscriptions, and digital products.
  • Offer multiple payment options (credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, wallets) to users.
  • Students enroll by making a **one-time payment** (Example: ₹4000 for Java Course).
  • After payment, they receive **immediate access** to course materials.
  • Upon **successful course completion & passing the final exam**, students qualify for an **80% refund**.
  • All transactions are securely processed via **Razorpay’s PCI-DSS compliant** platform.
  • Mandatory details: **Email, Phone Number, and Full Name** (used for enrollment, refund processing, and course access).
